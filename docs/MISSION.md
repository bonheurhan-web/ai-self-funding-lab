# Mission

Prove that an AI-operated company can create sustainable internet income by repeatedly identifying narrow problems, shipping useful solutions, measuring demand, and allocating resources based on evidence.

## Primary objective

`Portfolio Monthly Revenue >= ChatGPT Pro monthly price x 5`

The target uses the current public monthly price of ChatGPT Pro at the time of each report. The report must record the price used so the threshold remains auditable.

## Operating model

- ChatGPT sets strategy, allocates resources, and decides whether projects continue.
- Codex acts as CTO and owns engineering, automation, reliability, and technical delivery.
- The Chairman approves deployment, spending, account access, and other consequential external actions.

## Principles

1. Revenue is the final proof; traffic and usage are leading indicators.
2. Small validated bets are preferred to large speculative builds.
3. Every project must be independently measurable and disposable.
4. Shared infrastructure must reduce repeated work without coupling projects.
5. Costs, failures, and stopped projects must be reported honestly.
6. Decisions must be recorded with evidence and a review date.
7. Monetization must follow [`MONETIZATION.md`](MONETIZATION.md): validate demand before building payment infrastructure and prefer approved low-friction Phase 1 methods.
