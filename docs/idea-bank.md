# Idea Bank

This file records Chairman-proposed project and process ideas. Items here are not approved projects.
They require separate review before validation, development, spending, publishing, or deployment.

## 1. Gemini user simulation review process

- Type: process idea
- Status: `process_candidate`

### Purpose

Use Gemini to simulate external target users and critique a page, product copy, price, credibility,
and purchase intent. The goal is to avoid internal echo-chamber approval.

### Initial use

Use this after the `ai-workflow-library` minimum presale page is complete as an external simulation
review step.

### Follow-up action

Before preview deployment, create `USER_SIMULATION.md` defining 3-5 simulated user personas and the
review questions they should answer.

- Type: project idea
- Status: `project_candidate`

### Positioning

Produce and assist with Xiaohongshu content operations around Codex, AI automation, AI side
businesses, and the AI self-funding experiment.

- What Codex is

Do not develop this now. It is only a second-project candidate.

## 3. AI Startup Simulator

- Type: future project idea
- Status: `future_project_candidate`

### Positioning

If `ai-workflow-library` proves effective, build an AI business simulation game based on real
experiment methods and data. The player acts as the owner of an AI company and makes decisions about

### Dependency

Reassess only after `ai-workflow-library` completes at least one launch and review cycle.
