# Mission

Build a portfolio of small, useful software products that generate enough revenue to fund their own operation and the next round of experiments.

## First principles

1. Ship useful products quickly.
2. Validate demand with measurable traffic, usage, or revenue.
3. Keep operating costs low and architecture simple.
4. Automate repeatable engineering and reporting work.
5. Continue, change, or stop projects based on evidence.

## Current objective

Launch the first product, Micro Dev Tools, with a focused set of developer utilities and acquire organic search traffic through useful, indexable pages.

## Decision model

- ChatGPT sets strategy and allocates resources.
- Codex owns technical execution and operational reliability.
- The Chairman approves deployments, spending, and other consequential external actions.
